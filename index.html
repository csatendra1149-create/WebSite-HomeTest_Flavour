About HomeTest Flavours

A smart food delivery platform designed for Nepali professionals, office workers, and families

HomeTest Flavours is revolutionizing food delivery in Nepal by connecting home kitchens with workplaces. We deliver fresh, homemade meals across major cities like Birgunj and Kathmandu—bringing the taste.and comfort of home straight to your office.No more missing home-cooked meals during your busy workday!
